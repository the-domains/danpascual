In the gaily flamboyant footsteps of 'Zombadings'

PARDON filmmaker Lemuel Lorca if he has decided to display his CineFilipino best film trophy (for "Ned's Project") in his bedroom-making it the last thing he sees at night and the first thing he sees in the morning. The award is special to him because it's his first local recognition.
