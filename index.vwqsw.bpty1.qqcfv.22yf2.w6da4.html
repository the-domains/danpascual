'Paki', 'Changing Partners' top 2017 Cinema One Originals awards

MANILA -- "Paki" and "Changing Partners" bagged the most awards at the 2017 Cinema One Originals Film Festival awards night held at the Dolphy Theater in Quezon City Sunday.
